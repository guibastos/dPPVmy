Where I'm from

I am originally from Brazil. I grew up in a city in the south of Brazil called Curitiba. When you think of Brazil, you think of warm weather, beaches and happy people drinking beer. Well, Curitiba is not exactly that. It is a nice, well organized city, but cold and super humid. My uncle had a Siberian Husky who slept outside and could not survive Curitiba’s weather. She died in a cold winter night. A breed that can thrive on extreme temperatures of Alaska and Northern Europe, could not survive Curitiba’s cold and humid winter nights.

More About Me
